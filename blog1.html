Experience the Thrill of Off-Road Adventures

Start your Antigua journey the right way with convenient airport transfer and car rental services from V.C. Bird International Airport. As soon as you arrive, you can enjoy a smooth pickup or private transfer to your destination—or collect your rental car and begin exploring the island at your own pace.

One of the top places to visit is Nelson’s Dockyard, located in the beautiful English Harbour. Just a 30-minute drive from the airport, this historic gem is easily accessible whether you choose a direct airport transfer or prefer the flexibility of a self-drive car rental.

Powerful Performance on Any Terrain

With a reliable airport transfer service, you can sit back and relax as you’re driven through scenic coastal roads to your hotel or straight to the dockyard. It’s a stress-free option, especially after a long flight, ensuring comfort, safety, and timely arrival. For travelers who value convenience, this is the perfect way to begin your island experience.

On the other hand, opting for a car rental service gives you complete freedom to explore Antigua beyond the usual tourist spots. After picking up your vehicle at the airport, you can take a leisurely drive to Nelson’s Dockyard, stopping along the way to enjoy hidden beaches, local food spots, and breathtaking viewpoints.

Designed for Off-Road Enthusiasts

At Nelson’s Dockyard, you’ll discover a UNESCO World Heritage Site rich in history and charm. Once home to Horatio Nelson, this restored naval base now features museums, boutique shops, restaurants, and a lively marina filled with luxury yachts. It’s the perfect mix of culture and relaxation.

Enhance your visit by taking a short drive up to Shirley Heights, where you’ll be rewarded with panoramic views of the harbor. If you plan your trip right, you can also enjoy the famous Sunday sunset party—an experience not to be missed.

Combining airport transfer and car rental services allows you to enjoy the best of both worlds. You can start with a comfortable transfer upon arrival and later switch to a rental car for flexible island exploration. This approach ensures a seamless travel experience tailored to your needs.

Whether you prefer to relax with a private transfer or take control of your itinerary with a rental car, getting to Nelson’s Dockyard is easy and enjoyable. With well-connected roads and stunning scenery along the way, your journey becomes just as memorable as the destination itself.

Make the most of your time in Antigua by choosing the right travel options. From the moment you land at V.C. Bird International Airport to your visit to Nelson’s Dockyard, every step of your journey can be smooth, comfortable, and filled with discovery.
