Rental Terms and Conditions

Before booking a vehicle or chauffeur service with us, please take a moment to review our rental terms and conditions outlined below. By making a reservation with Sandy Feet, you agree to abide by these terms and conditions.

  1. Driver’s License and Age Requirements:

    • All drivers must present a valid driver's license at the time of rental.
    • The minimum age to rent a vehicle with Sandy Feet is 25 years old.
    • Drivers under the age of 25 may be subject to additional fees and restrictions.

  2. Reservation and Payment:

    • Reservations can be made online through our website, via phone, or in person at our office.
    • A valid credit card is required to secure your reservation.
    • Payment is due in full at the time of vehicle pickup.
    • We accept major credit cards, including Visa, Mastercard, and American Express.

  3. Rental Period:

    • The rental period begins and ends at the dates and times specified in your reservation.
    • Vehicles must be returned to the designated location by the agreed-upon return time.
    • Late returns may be subject to additional fees.

  4. Vehicle Use and Restrictions:

    • The rented vehicle must only be driven by authorized drivers listed on the rental agreement.
    • The vehicle must be used in accordance with local traffic laws and regulations.
    • Smoking and pets are prohibited inside the rental vehicles.

  5. Insurance and Liability:

    • All rental vehicles are covered by basic insurance, which includes collision and liability coverage.
    • Additional insurance options may be available for purchase at the time of rental.
    • Renters are responsible for any damage or loss to the vehicle, including theft, vandalism, and accidents.

  6. Fuel Policy:

    • Vehicles are provided with a full tank of fuel at the time of rental.
    • Renters are responsible for returning the vehicle with the same level of fuel.
    • A refueling fee will apply if the vehicle is returned with less fuel than at the time of rental.

  7. Cancellation and Modifications:

    • Cancellations or modifications to reservations must be made at least 24 hours in advance.
    • Failure to cancel or modify a reservation within the specified time frame may result in fees or penalties.

  8. Amendments to Terms and Conditions:

    • Sandy Feet reserves the right to amend these rental terms and conditions at any time.
    • Any changes will be communicated to customers in advance.
